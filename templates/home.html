HarvestBud App

Your AI farm insights buddy provider

The HarvestBud App is an AI-local language enabled with augmented farm advisory that helps farmer folks for their farm featuring advisory on agricultural inputs, weather forecast and crop management that can enable farm locations minimizing cost and maximizing crop production which contribute to food and water security in the context of sustainability and climate change. Read more.

Sorjan Integrated Farm

(Zero Waste Farm Production)

The project is based in the northern part of Luzon that helps promote integrated farming covering a zero waste prodction and IoT enabled precision farming which will significantly increase production. The integrated farm also serve as an agro-tourism farm where high valued crops attract tourist for free picking Read more.

Farm 4.0 Insight Provider

Your One Stop Farm Insight Provider

The Farm 4.0 Insight Provider program is a project that helps farmers to understand the different technology that can be maximize in their farm in order to maximize their productivity. One example for this is the HarvestBud App that provides advisory for the farmers and the Integrated Farm promoting zero-waste production Read more.
